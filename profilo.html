About me

I am an IT professional with a strong focus on technology leadership, cybersecurity and the evolution of industrial systems.

I work at the intersection of business strategy, infrastructure, applications, data, security and production, with the goal of turning technology into a practical driver of competitiveness, resilience and growth.

My approach reflects a CTO mindset: understanding business needs and translating them into clear priorities, sustainable architectures, reliable services and measurable outcomes.

I also apply a CISO perspective, treating cybersecurity, risk management and business continuity as essential components of every technology decision.

Professional mission

To build technology ecosystems capable of supporting reliable operations, informed decisions and organizational growth.

  • Improving the resilience of infrastructure and digital services.
  • Integrating enterprise IT and industrial environments securely.
  • Supporting production through reliable systems, data and processes.
  • Defining clear and sustainable technology roadmaps.
  • Developing skills, accountability and organizational capabilities.

The role of technology

Technology creates value when it is understandable, governed, secure and genuinely adopted by people.

For this reason, I consider governance, communication, capability development and change management just as important as technical architecture and implementation.
