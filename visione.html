Technology Leadership

My CTO Vision

Technology should not simply support the business. It should become one of the primary drivers of competitiveness, innovation and sustainable growth.

Technology as Business Strategy

The role of a modern CTO goes far beyond infrastructure, applications and projects.

Technology leadership means creating alignment between business goals, industrial operations, cybersecurity, people and innovation. Every technological decision must generate measurable value for the organization.

Leadership Principles

Business First

Technology must solve business problems. Not create new ones.

Security by Design

Cybersecurity is not a project. It is a permanent business capability.

Industrial Excellence

Production systems require stability, visibility and continuous optimization.

People Enable Technology

Digital transformation succeeds only when people understand and adopt change.

Five Strategic Priorities

  1. Business Alignment
  2. Operational Resilience
  3. Cybersecurity Governance
  4. Industrial Digitalization
  5. Continuous Innovation

Technology Domains

MES, SCADA, OT Networks, Production Data, Automation.

Cybersecurity

Governance, Risk, Compliance, Business Continuity.

Data & AI

Analytics, Artificial Intelligence, Decision Support.

Leadership

Teams, Processes, Organization, Change Management.

Innovation

Emerging Technologies, Digital Roadmaps, Continuous Improvement.

My Vision in One Sentence

Connecting technology, cybersecurity and industrial systems to enable production excellence and organizational growth.
