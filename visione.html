Technology leadership

My CTO Vision

Technology should not simply support the business. It should enable competitiveness, resilience, production excellence and sustainable growth.

Technology as business strategy

The role of a modern CTO goes far beyond infrastructure, applications and project delivery.

Technology leadership means creating alignment between business goals, industrial operations, cybersecurity, people, data and innovation.

Every technology decision should contribute to measurable organizational value, stronger operational capabilities and better long-term resilience.

Leadership principles

Business first

Technology must address real business and operational needs, rather than create unnecessary complexity.

Security by design

Cybersecurity must be embedded into architecture, processes, governance and organizational behavior.

Industrial excellence

Production systems require reliability, visibility, integration and continuous optimization.

People enable technology

Digital transformation succeeds when people understand, adopt and improve the new way of working.

Strategic priorities

Business alignment

Connecting technology investments and priorities with business strategy and operational objectives.

Operational resilience

Strengthening continuity, recoverability and the ability to respond effectively to disruption.

Cybersecurity governance

Managing cyber risk through clear responsibilities, informed decisions and sustainable controls.

Industrial digitalization

Improving production processes through connected, secure and reliable digital systems.

Data-driven decisions

Turning reliable information into better operational and strategic decision-making.

Continuous innovation

Exploring new technologies with discipline, measurable objectives and responsible governance.

Technology domains

Enterprise IT

Infrastructure, cloud, workplace, applications, architecture and service governance.

Industrial systems

Manufacturing applications, operational technology, production data and IT/OT integration.

Cybersecurity

Governance, risk management, resilience, business continuity and security culture.

Data and AI

Analytics, artificial intelligence, governance and decision support.

Organization

Teams, capabilities, operating models, accountability and change management.

Innovation

Emerging technologies, digital roadmaps, experimentation and continuous improvement.

CTO and CISO mindset

Sustainable technology leadership requires both opportunity awareness and risk awareness.

A CTO perspective focuses on innovation, scalability, performance and business value.

A CISO perspective ensures that security, resilience, compliance and risk remain integral to every decision.

Combining these two perspectives supports technology choices that are ambitious, responsible and sustainable.

My vision in one sentence

Connecting technology, cybersecurity and industrial systems to enable production excellence and organizational growth.
